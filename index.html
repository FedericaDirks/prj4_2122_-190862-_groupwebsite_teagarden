Tea Garden

Tea Garden is a Tea shop and restaurant. We provide a wide range of biological and fairtrade tea made by experts. Whether you prefer black tea, herbal tea or Yellow tea Tea Garden has the richets flavours. Feel free to stop by and take a cup.

Looking for something to go with the tea? Our restaurant also offers cakes, cupcakes, scones & sandwiches. Tea Garden also offers high tea arrangements. No time for tea? No problem, Tea Garden also offer packaged tea you can enjoy at home.
